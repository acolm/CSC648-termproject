Who I am

I am a 21-year-old American-Indian born in California, United States. I moved back to India when I was in 3rd grade, and returned to that states a year ago when I transferred to SF State.



My Experience

In India, I have the opportunity to have a multitude of experiences. Among my professional experiences there, I worked for a year and half with an organization focused on enhancing global connections. We did this by creating internship opportunities in our city and then connect them to students and recent graduates of other countries. During my time here, I also had the opportunity to host a large scale event surrounding global cultures. I also had an internship with a IT company located in the city that gave me a lot of hands on experience.



Where I am now

Currently, I am a student in San Francisco State University’s B.S. in Computer Science program. As mentioned before, I recently transferred here from India. As I spent some of my childhood here, it was luckily easy for me to adjust. Currently I'm also working part-time as a Project Manager and applying to various internship opportunities.
