Who I am

I am a 24-year-old Hispanic veteran born in Lima, Peru. I spent the majority of my preadolescence days living and studying in my country of origin alongside my siblings. In 2004, I traveled to the United States seeking better economic and educational opportunities otherwise not easily available in Peru. I learned English and took on a newfound hobby in playing classical music with my flute until my 2014 graduation from Carlmont High School located in Belmont, CA



My Experience

I enlisted in the U.S. Marine Corps at the age of 18. My original military occupation specialty was Supply Administration Specialist. However, I spent my military contract working in various areas including in the maintenance of weapons, as a Customer Service representative, a Quality Assurance supervisor, an Additive Manufacturing supervisor, and a Supply and Warehouse manager. However, my most valuable experience in the military was during an overseas deployment while being the team lead of a group of Engineers to be the Security Forces for Camp Manion at Al-Taqaddum Air Base, Iraq. This role was a new and dangerous position for the team, including myself, but we were able to successfully accomplish our mission with the mentorship and assistance of our leaders and comrades. I finished my military contract as of July of 2019.
